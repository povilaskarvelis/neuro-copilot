description: Use when executing steps that need trial safety, regulatory label warnings, and post-market adverse-event signals interpreted together without overclaiming.
---

Use this skill when the active step is about safety, tolerability, warnings, adverse events, or benefit-risk interpretation.

Instructions:
1. Distinguish trial safety findings, current label warnings, and post-market reporting signals; they answer different questions.
2. Do not treat FAERS or openFDA report counts as incidence rates or proof of causality.
3. Do not treat the absence of a boxed warning as proof of low risk, or the presence of one as a quantitative estimate of risk.
4. When comparing safety across products, align the evidence types being compared.
5. Load `references/safety-risk-execution.md` before concluding safety-heavy steps.
