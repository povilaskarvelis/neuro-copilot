Use this reference when executing safety- or risk-focused steps.

- Use `get_clinical_trial` for trial-record safety context and study design.
- Use `get_dailymed_drug_label` for current US warning language.
- Use `search_fda_adverse_events` for post-market signal context, not incidence.
- Keep label language, trial observations, and surveillance signals distinct in the summary.
- Be explicit about what kind of risk statement the evidence supports and what it does not support.
