name: clinical-trials-execution
description: Use when executing trial-centered steps that require disciplined status, phase, outcome, sponsor, or safety interpretation.
---

Use this skill when the active step is mainly about ClinicalTrials.gov records, trial landscape statistics, drug-label context, or post-market safety signals.

Instructions:
1. Separate discovery from inspection. Use trial search or landscape tools first, then inspect the most relevant NCT records directly.
2. Treat recruiting, completed, terminated, and active-not-recruiting states as distinct evidence categories.
3. Do not infer efficacy, regulatory approval, or current clinical relevance from phase alone.
4. When safety interpretation matters, check label or FAERS context explicitly rather than relying only on trial summaries.
5. Load `references/clinical-trials-execution-playbook.md` before concluding trial-heavy steps.
