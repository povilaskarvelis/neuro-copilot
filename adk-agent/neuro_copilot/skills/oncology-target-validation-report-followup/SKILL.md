---

Use this skill when a follow-up question asks for clarification of target quality, vulnerability selectivity, biomarker context, or tractability for a cancer target mentioned in the report.

Instructions:
1. Keep the lookup anchored to the target and cancer context already under discussion.
2. Distinguish dependency, selectivity, and tractability rather than treating them as one concept.
3. Prefer one or two decisive follow-up lookups rather than reopening a broad pan-cancer scan.
4. Be explicit when the evidence points to pan-essentiality, lineage specificity, or weak translational support.
5. Load `references/oncology-followup-playbook.md` before answering oncology target-validation follow-ups.
