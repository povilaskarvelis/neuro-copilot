Use this reference when the plan is safety- or risk-centered.

- Use `search_clinical_trials` and `get_clinical_trial` for trial-era safety and study context.
- Use `get_dailymed_drug_label` for current US warning language and formal precautions.
- Use `search_fda_adverse_events` for post-market signal context rather than confirmed incidence.
- Keep trial adverse events, label warnings, and spontaneous-report signals separate in the plan.
